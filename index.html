I consider myself a very versatile and adaptable person, which is why I have always been interested in many different fields. From outdoor activities to indoor ones, I have, since ever, been curious to try out as many areas as possible. I love learning and my goal in life is to be capable of doing as many things as possible, whether it be in the field of technology, sports, music or anything else.

I played football up until 7th grade quite competitively, but nowadays I am trying out new sports like skating. Music plays a key role in everyone's life and I am no exception. Recently, in 2019, I decided to pick up the guitar and start learning it by myself and now it is an essential part of my daily life.

I am ambitious and dedicated. Everything that I put my mind into, I will do my absolute best to achieve it. I do not back out of a challenge and most importantly, I am teachable.

Regarding my professional life, my main interest is AI and Machine Learning. In 2022 I took a course on Machine Learning which caught the whole of my attention. Everything about it interests me and I could not be more excited to further deepen my knoweldge in this field.
